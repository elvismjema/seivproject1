Why Choose Computer Science?

Computer Science opens doors to creativity, innovation, and problem-solving. It gives you the tools to design software, understand technology, and solve real-world challenges.

With a CS degree, you can enter fields like software engineering, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, or data science. The opportunities are wide and in high demand.
